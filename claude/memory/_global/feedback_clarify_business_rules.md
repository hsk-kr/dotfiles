---
name: Clarify ambiguous requirements early
description: Ask about business logic and design decisions before implementing, not after
type: feedback
---

Don't assume business rules. Ask explicitly when requirements are ambiguous.

**Why:** Assumptions lead to rework. Better to ask one question upfront than rebuild after.

**How to apply:** When adding features with payment models, data flows, or user-facing behavior, confirm the expected behavior before writing code.
